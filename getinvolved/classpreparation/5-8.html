Age group 5 - 8

Preparation

Classes for this age group usually have a duration of around 30-45 min. However at the new world centers the classes are around 1:30 h. These classes are taught in the same format as the kids of age group 2-4 but there are changes with regards to what and how the classes are taught. In the 1:30 h classes we use course material from English books e.g. Family and Friends 2-6 (author Naomi Simmons). And in the 45 min classes we use course material from the English books Tieng Anh (4-8) by authors Bo Giao Duc and Va Dao Tao.

Warm-up

Warm up, is comprised of physical exercise (click here for an example) and songs (click here for an example). Furthermore, a round of questions is added, where some basic English questions and answers are taught to students for example: what time is it ? what is the date ? how are you feeling ?. Some answers to those questions are also taught to the students. In the 1:30h classes, usually more time is spent on questions and more comprehensive questions are asked. And since the 1:30 h classes are usually smaller, focus is put on each student in the class.

Teaching

After the warm-up round the teacher then moves on to the teaching part of the class. Similarly to other age-groups (2-4) flashcards are taught in this age-group. Flashcards are provided beforehand by the Vietnamese teachers at the new world center, so the volunteer does not have to prepare those. The volunteer can also incorporate different ways of teaching flashcards (click here for example) instead of just repetition, like for example through games (click here for an example). The 1:30h classes are based on the content of the English materials (books) provided by the center. In the 1:30 h classes, more games are incorporated in the classes for example quizzing the kids on the flashcards (e.g. asking them to describe the what they see on the flash card), or creating and reciting poems during class related to the topic but also singing songs.

Games

The 30-45 minutes classes usually end with playing games (see list of games) and singing goodbye a song (click here for an example). In the 1:30 h classes games and songs are incorporated throughout the class so it is up to the volunteer to decide how he/she is going to teach the class.
