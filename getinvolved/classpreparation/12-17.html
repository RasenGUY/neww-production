Age group 12 - 17

Preparation

Classes of this age group usually have a duration of 45 minutes and 1:30h. The 45 min classes are generallu taught in state school, which means that you will be traveling around 45m (back and forth). The 1:30h classes are classes that are taught at the new world schools. These are topic classes which means that you will be preparing a topic to teach in the class. In the 1:30 h classes we use course material from different ESL websites and facebook groups for guidance. And in the 45 min classes we use course material from the English books Tieng Anh (4-8) by authors Bo Giao Duc and Va Dao Tao.

Warm up

Classes normally start with introducing the kids to basic questions like for example how are you? what's your name? how many people are in your family? who is the youngest? Etc. (see list). In the 1:30 h classes more questions can be asked like: how was your week, weekend etc?. Some students of some classes also enjoy singing songs, we have created a list of songs you can sing with the kids, note however that not all of the kids enjoy singing songs, you could ask before hand if the kids are in the mood for singing a song. Next, some volunteers incoporate physical excercise in their warm-up routine (click here for and example).

Teaching

The main part of the 45 minutes class is based on the lesson plan that the Vietnamese teachers at New World, whom derive their plan from the contents of the English we books used in the state schools. Lesson plans just present the topic and some tips on how to teach the topic however, you can choose yourself on how you are going to teach the topic (see example). Furthermore sometimes volunteers also bring music instruments like guitars to class (see here). The 1:30 classes are topic classes, in these classes you can choose what to teach. There are many online sources you can use for inspiration on which topic to teach and how you can teach.
