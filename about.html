Who We Are!

The New World International English School is an organization set up to benefit kids of all ages set in the northern part of Vietnam. Located in the main city of Thai Binh district, New world tries its best to provide very affordable english lessons to Vietnamese children in and around Thai Binh City. It achieves this through the application of an array of games, workshops and lessons, with the help from a number of foreign volunteers. New World sets itself apart from other English teaching centers in Vietnam in that we host the largest number of foreign English speaking volunteers in Thai Binh City.
