Who We Are!

The New World International English School is an organization set up to benefit kids of all ages set in the northern part of Vietnam. Hidden in the city of Thai Binh district, New world tries its best to support Vietnamese kids with learning the English language. It achieves this through the application of an array of games, workshops and lessons, with the help from a medley of foreign volunteers. New World sets itself apart from other English teaching centers in Vietnam in that it houses the largest amount of foreign English speaking volunteers in Thai Binh city.

History

New World International English School started 5 years ago. Trained as an English teacher, the director was confronted by many students telling her that they can’t find a good job because their level of English was really poor. Understanding this and also considering the success of her own children, Mrs. Hong decided then to open the New World International English School. The first center was opened in Kiến Xương, Thai Binh. Three months later a new center was opened in Thái Thụy, Thai Binh. After that another center was opened in Tien Hai, however this center was not successful and was thus closed. Mrs. Hong then proceeded to open another center in Thai Binh city (the city center of Thai Binh) this center was really successful and after three years operation a new center was opened this year in Thai Binh city as well this center (this is center is now the main center and is the place where most of the English classes are being taught).

Our Mission

New World’s mission is to provide very affordable English lessons for students regardless of their social background (rich and poor). New World recognizes the importance of the English language and the opportunity it provides for people in the labor market that’s why it provides affordable English lessons for all people regardless of age.
